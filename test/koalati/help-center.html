Frequently asked questions

What's an "active project"?

Projects are considered active when automated tools have been used to test the project during the current month.

Newly created project will always be considered active for the current month, as they are scanned automatically upon creation.

Projects that are scanned and tested by the quality monitoring system (which will be made available soon)¬†will also be considered active.

Even if an active project is deleted, it will still count towards your quota for the month, as it was active during this period. Creating a new project with the same website URL will not count as a new active project, as it is the same website. However, changing the URL of an active project for that of another website which is not already an active project of yours will count as a new active project.

How many projects can I have?

As many as you like!

There is no limit to the number of projects you can have; only active projects are counted towards your monthly quota.

This allows you to keep an archive of your status and progress in the quality control process, and to pick up where you left off whenever you want.‚Äç

Can I join a team as a free user?

Yes! Every user can join teams.

Joining a team will give you access to the features and quotas of that team, but only for team projects. Your personal projects will still be limited to your plan's quotas and feature set.

How do team quotas work?

Team projects count towards the quotas of the team's owner. This quota is shared by the owner's personal projects and their team's projects.

This means that a user with a quota of 100 active projects could have, for example:

  • 80 team projects and 20 personal projects;
  • 100 team projects and no personal projects;
  • 0 team projects and 100 personal projects.

Team owners can still be participant of other teams of which they are not the owner. The projects in those other teams do not count toward their own quota.

How many teams can I create?

Each account with team creation privilege can create one team.

If you need to create more than one team for your business, we recommend creating multiple accounts.

Are there plans with higher quotas?

There are not at the moment, but you can contact us at info@koalati.com and we'll see what we can do to help you.
